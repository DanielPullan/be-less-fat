Please bear in mind that BMI calculations have flaws. It doesn't take into account your age (if you're an adult), your gender or your muscle mass. Whilst it can be used as a general guide, it's not the be-end-all in saying whether you are a healthy weight or in good health otherwise.

If you have an eating disorder, please do not use Be Less Fat. Please consult medical advice.
